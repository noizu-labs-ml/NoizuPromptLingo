# AI Templates Sub-Skill

**Generate and monetize prompt libraries, automation workflows, and MCP servers as digital products.**

---

## Overview

The AI Templates sub-skill transforms technical expertise into revenue-generating digital products. From prompt libraries to custom MCP servers, this stream focuses on tools and workflows that solve specific problems for a target audience.

**Core Value Proposition:**
- Solve high-value problems in underserved niches
- Package as premium products ($97-$497 price range)
- Deliver instantly (digital product, no fulfillment)
- Achieves revenue with minimal ongoing support

## Revenue Model

- **One-time purchases:** $27-497 per sale
- **Subscription tier:** $9-49/month for platform access
- **Volume strategy:** High margin, relatively low volume (5-50 sales/month per product)

**Typical conversion funnel:**

## Key Workflows

### 1. Niche Research & Validation
**Duration:** 3-5 hours

1. Identify problem space (pain point, audience size, willingness to pay)
2. Estimate market size and competition
3. Find your angle (differentiation)
4. Validate via audience/community feedback

**File:** `NICHE-RESEARCH.md`

### 2. Product Development
**Duration:** 1-4 weeks

1. Define product scope (what's included, what's out)
2. Create core material (prompts, workflows, code)
3. Build supporting assets (guide, examples, use cases)
4. Polish and package

**File:** `AGENT.md` - use "Full Development" prompt pattern

### 3. Launch & Monetization
**Duration:** 1-2 weeks

1. Create sales page (landing page, product preview, testimonials)
2. Choose platform (Gumroad recommended for V1)
3. Set pricing (test $47-97 for first product)
4. Promote to relevant communities

### 4. Performance Tracking
**Ongoing:**

1. Monitor sales velocity and conversions
2. Track customer feedback and refund rate
3. Identify bestsellers and dogs
4. Plan V2 improvements or new products

**File:** `PROJECT-TRACKER.md`

## Platform Selection

### Primary Launch: Gumroad
- **Why:** Lowest friction, built-in discovery, payments handled
- **Audience:** Creators, makers, technical audience
- **Fees:** 10% + payment processing
- **Time to revenue:** Hours (after product is ready)

### Growth: Stan Store
- **Why:** Better for product bundles, higher price points
- **Fees:** $29-99/month + variable
- **When to move:** 3-5 products, $500+/month revenue

### Scale: Own Site (Lemonsqueezy or Stripe)
- **Why:** Full control, lower fees (5-8%), better unit economics
- **When to move:** $2,000+/month revenue threshold
- **Effort:** More technical setup, ongoing maintenance

---

## Pricing Strategy

### First Product (V1)
- **Price:** $47 (accessible, validates market)
- **Goal:** 20-50 sales in first month
- **Success:** Refund rate < 10%, positive feedback

### Second+ Products
- **Price testing:** $27 (volume play), $97 (premium), $197 (high-value)
- **Position:** Premium products for underserved niches
- **Avoid:** Race-to-bottom pricing

### Subscription Tier (After 3+ products)
- **Monthly access:** $9-29/month
- **Value:** Early access, bundle discounts, private community
- **Expected:** 5-10% of customer base at $15/month = $100-300/month passive

## Failure Points & How to Fix

| Problem | Signal | Fix |
|---------|--------|-----|
| No sales | 0 sales in first 2 weeks | Poor positioning/targeting or no audience |
| High refund rate | >20% refunds | Product not meeting expectations; revise promise |
| Stagnant traffic | No clicks despite promotion | Landing page not compelling; A/B test headlines |
| Low conversion | <3% click-to-purchase | Weak sales copy or unclear value; rewrite page |
| Community fatigue | Declining engagement | Promoting too frequently; space launches 2-3 weeks apart |
