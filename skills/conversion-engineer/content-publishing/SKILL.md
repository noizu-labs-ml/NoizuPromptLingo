# Content Publishing Sub-Skill

**Build authority and recurring revenue through technical writing, newsletters, video courses, and thought leadership.**

---

## Overview

The Content Publishing sub-skill transforms expertise into sustainable income through evergreen content that attracts organic traffic, builds an audience, and generates revenue through subscriptions, sponsorships, and course sales.

**Core Value Proposition:**
- Build SEO authority (compounds over time)
- Create recurring revenue (subscriptions)
- Attract sponsorship opportunities
- Establish credibility for related products/services
- Achieve passive income as content ages

## Revenue Model

**Primary streams:**
1. **Newsletter subscriptions** (70% - recurring, most stable)
2. **Sponsorships** (15% - as audience grows)
3. **Course/product sales** (15% - one-time)

**Typical growth curve:**
- Month 1-2: 0 revenue (building audience)
- Month 2-3: $0-500 (first paid subscribers)
- Month 3-6: $500-2,000 (audience growing, sponsorships)
- Month 6-12: $2,000-5,000 (established, compounding)

## Workflow Overview

### 1. Content Strategy & Research
**Duration:** 1-2 weeks

1. Identify your expertise niche
2. Keyword research (high volume, low competition)
3. Content calendar planning (12-week roadmap)
4. Platform selection (Dev.to primary, cross-post to Medium/Hashnode)

**File:** `KEYWORD-RESEARCH.md`

### 2. Content Creation
**Duration:** 2-4 weeks per piece

1. Research and outline
2. Write first draft (4-8 hours)
3. Self-edit and fact-check
4. Add examples, code, visuals
5. Final polish and publication

**File:** `AGENT.md` - "Full Development" pattern

### 3. Distribution & Promotion
**Duration:** 1 week per piece

1. Publish on primary platform (Dev.to)
2. Cross-post to Medium, Hashnode, LinkedIn
3. Share in relevant communities (Reddit, HN, Twitter)
4. Engage with comments
5. Track engagement metrics

### 4. Monetization Setup
**Months 2-3 after publishing starts:**

1. Launch paid newsletter tier on Substack
2. Repurpose content into courses
3. Attract sponsorship partnerships
4. Build email list (free tier)

**File:** `PROJECT-TRACKER.md`

## Content Quality Standards

**Technical accuracy:**
- ✅ Code examples tested and working
- ✅ Claims backed by sources or experience
- ✅ Up-to-date with latest versions (2026)

**Presentation:**
- ✅ Clear headline (benefit-driven, not clickbait)
- ✅ Introduction hook (why reader should care)
- ✅ Logical flow (easy to follow)
- ✅ Visual aids (diagrams, code blocks, screenshots)
- ✅ Call-to-action (newsletter signup, related post, course)

**SEO optimization:**
- ✅ Primary keyword in title and first 100 words
- ✅ Headings with keywords where natural
- ✅ Meta description (155 chars, compelling)
- ✅ Internal/external links (3-5 per 1000 words)

## Success Metrics

### Month 1-2 (Foundation)
- ✅ Published 4-8 pieces
- ✅ 500+ total views across pieces
- ✅ Average engagement rate >3% (shares, comments)
- ✅ Email list: 50-100 subscribers

### Month 3-6 (Growth)
- ✅ Published 12-16 pieces (consistent 1-2/week)
- ✅ 5,000+ monthly views
- ✅ Email list: 200-500 subscribers
- ✅ 10-20 paid subscribers ($50-500/month)
- ✅ 1-2 sponsorship inquiries

### Month 6-12 (Scale)
- ✅ Published 24-30 pieces
- ✅ 20,000+ monthly views
- ✅ Email list: 1,000+ subscribers
- ✅ 50-100+ paid subscribers ($500-2,500/month)
- ✅ 2-4 active sponsorships ($500-2K/month)
- ✅ Course or guide product launched

## Sponsorship & Monetization

### Getting Sponsorships

**Minimum qualifications:**
- 100+ newsletter subscribers OR
- 5,000+ monthly blog views OR
- 500+ engaged community

**How to pitch sponsors:**
1. Create a "sponsor us" page with stats and rates
2. Reach out to relevant companies (tools in your niche)
3. Offer flexible pricing ($200-2K/month depending on reach)
4. Provide sponsor with detailed metrics

**Types of sponsorships:**
- Newsletter: $500-2K per feature (1-3 per newsletter)
- Blog: $300-1K per post placement
- Video: $500-2K per pre-roll or integration

## Content Repurposing Strategy

```
Blog Post (4 hours)
  ├→ Newsletter issue (30 mins - edited/expanded)
  ├→ Social media threads (30 mins - 5-10 tweets)
  ├→ LinkedIn article (15 mins - cross-post)
  └→ Video script (1 hour - record + edit)
       ├→ YouTube video (2 hours - film + edit)
       ├→ TikTok clips (1 hour - short form)
       └→ Course module (4 hours - flesh out)
```

**ROI:** 5-8 formats from single piece of content.

## Common Pitfalls & Solutions

| Problem | Signal | Solution |
|---------|--------|----------|
| No consistency | Publishing erratic, months between posts | Create 4-week content calendar, commit to schedule |
| No audience growth | Views/subscribers flat | Check SEO; improve titles and CTAs |
| Low quality feedback | Poor engagement, few comments | Step up research depth, add visuals, test new formats |
| Burnout | Can't keep up with publishing | Scale back to 1 piece/week; focus on quality |
| No monetization | Great audience, zero revenue | Launch paid tier or course; add sponsorships |

## Platform-Specific Tips

### Dev.to
- Large developer audience, strong SEO
- Cross-post from own blog or write native
- Use tags effectively (max 4, relevant ones)
- Engage in comments (builds following)

### Substack
- Best for newsletters with personality
- Free tier for email list building
- Paid tier at $5-20/month (offering: exclusive content + community)
- Monthly cadence: weekly free + exclusive paid

### Medium
- Partner program revenue share
- Built-in discovery, but declining
- Best as cross-post destination
- Syndicates to broader audience
