# Personalized Implementation Roadmap

Use this prompt to generate your specific 90-day and 12-month roadmap.

---

## PROMPT: Generate My Passive Income Roadmap

Based on my situation and chosen stream(s), create a detailed implementation roadmap.

### My Situation:
- Chosen primary stream: [AI Templates / Content Publishing / POD]
- Secondary stream (if any): [None / AI Templates / Content Publishing / POD]
- Time available: [hours/week]
- Budget for ads/testing: [amount]
- First revenue target: [date]
- Year 1 revenue goal: [$X/month]
- Skills I have: [list]
- Skills I need to develop: [list]

### What I Need:

**1. 90-Day Implementation Plan**

Provide week-by-week breakdown for:
- Weeks 1-2: Foundation (research, skill development, setup)
- Weeks 3-4: Validation (test market demand, create sample product/content)
- Weeks 5-8: Build (create first product/content/designs at scale)
- Weeks 9-12: Launch & Optimize (go to market, get first customers, iterate)

For each phase:
- Daily/weekly time allocation
- Specific activities and deliverables
- Tools/skills needed
- Success milestones
- What to measure

**2. Platform Roadmap**

- Initial platform choice (Gumroad, Dev.to, Redbubble, etc.)
- When to expand to secondary platforms
- Ad platform launch timing (Google, Meta, etc.)
- When to build own site/platform

**3. Revenue Milestone Plan**

- What does first $100 revenue look like?
- What needs to happen to reach $500/month?
- Path to $1,000/month
- Scaling to $5,000/month

**4. Audience/Traffic Plan**

- How to get initial traffic/customers
- Organic growth strategy
- Paid ads strategy and budget allocation
- Community engagement approach

**5. Resource Requirements**

- Tools needed (free and paid)
- One-time costs vs. recurring costs
- Timeline for cost investment
- When costs become ROI-positive

**6. Risk Mitigation Timeline**

- Early warning signs of failure (what indicates I should pivot?)
- Decision points and Go/No-Go criteria
- Pivot points (when to change approach)
- Success validation (how do I know it's working?)

**7. Secondary Stream Integration (if applicable)**

- When to launch secondary stream
- How it synergizes with primary
- Resource reallocation plan
- Portfolio management approach

## Notes for Best Results

1. **Be specific:** "Increase sales" is vague. "Get 5 sales at $47 average" is actionable.
2. **Include time:** Every activity should have a time estimate.
3. **Define success:** What number/metric = success at each milestone?
4. **Plan for adjustment:** Your first plan won't be perfect; include checkpoints to adjust.
5. **Track everything:** Use the conversion-metrics skill to monitor progress weekly.
