# Monetization Strategy Assessment

Use this prompt to evaluate your strengths, constraints, and optimal income stream(s).

---

## PROMPT: Comprehensive Monetization Assessment

I want to develop a personalized passive income strategy. Please help me assess where I'll succeed.

### About Me (Please provide context for each):

**Skills Inventory:**
- Technical skills: [List any: coding, APIs, automation, software development, technical depth in specific areas]
- Creative skills: [List any: writing, design, visual thinking, brand development]
- Teaching/communication: [Teaching experience, comfort with audience interaction, community building]
- Business skills: [Marketing, sales, audience understanding, metrics tracking, etc.]

**Constraints:**
- Weekly time availability: [e.g., "5-8 hours/week", "10+ hours/week", "only weekends"]
- Financial resources: [e.g., "no budget", "$100-200/mo", "$500+/mo for ads"]
- Startup runway: [e.g., "need income in 2 months", "can invest 6 months", "12+ month horizon"]
- Risk tolerance: [Conservative, Moderate, or Aggressive]

**Current Situation:**
- Existing audience: [None, small community, established email list, large Twitter following, etc.]
- Current revenue streams: [None, freelancing, employment, part-time, etc.]
- Geographic/time zone: [Relevant for content timing/monetization]

**Goals:**
- Revenue target: [e.g., "$500/mo in Y1", "$1000/mo", "primary income eventually"]
- Timeline: [e.g., "first revenue in 4 weeks", "building for 12 months"]
- Work preference: [Batch work vs. consistent weekly work, geographic flexibility, etc.]

### Analysis Requested:

**1. Strengths Assessment**
- Which of the three streams (AI Templates, Content Publishing, POD) best align with my skills?
- What unique advantages do I have?
- What are my competitive differentiators?

**2. Constraints Mapping**
- Which streams fit my time/financial constraints?
- Which require the least startup investment from me?
- Which align with my risk tolerance?

**3. Timeline Reality Check**
- Given my constraints, when could I realistically see first revenue in each stream?
- How long until meaningful income ($1000+/month)?
- What's the longest I'd need to wait before knowing if a stream is working?

**4. Dependency Analysis**
- Do I have the prerequisite skills for my preferred stream?
- What skills would I need to develop?
- How long would skill development take?

**5. Recommendation**
- **Primary recommendation:** Which stream should I start with?
  - Why this stream?
  - What's the realistic timeline and revenue potential?
  - What should I do first (validation, setup, skill building)?

- **Secondary recommendation:** Should I eventually diversify?
  - Which stream would complement my primary choice?
  - When should I add it (after hitting revenue target, after 6 months, etc.)?
  - How would the streams work together?

**6. Risk Mitigation**
- What are my specific failure risks?
- How can I reduce them?
- What would success look like to validate I'm on the right track?
