## Platform Comparison

### AI Templates

| Platform | Cut | Audience | Best For |
|----------|-----|----------|----------|
| Gumroad | 10% | Creators, indie | Quick launch, testing |
| Stan Store | 5% | Creators | Lower fees |
| Etsy | 6.5%+fees | Broad | Volume, discovery |
| Own site | 3% (Stripe) | Direct traffic | Highest margin |

**Recommendation:** Start Gumroad → Move to own site at $2K/mo

### Content Publishing

| Platform | Model | Best For |
|----------|-------|----------|
| Substack | 10% of paid | Newsletter-first, community |
| Medium | Partner program | SEO, discoverability |
| Dev.to | Free | Developer credibility |
| Hashnode | Free | Technical authority |

**Recommendation:** Dev.to for SEO → Substack for monetization

### Print on Demand

| Platform | Base Cost | Best For |
|----------|-----------|----------|
| Printful | Higher | Quality, brand |
| Printify | Lower | Margin, variety |
| Redbubble | Marketplace | Discovery |
| TeePublic | Marketplace | Passive |

**Recommendation:** Redbubble for passive + Printify for branded
